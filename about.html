Jackson Spilsbury
is and independent video producer and
creative consultant based out of Tempe,
Arizona. He has directed and produced
visuals for many critically acclaimed artists,
stylistically catering to a wide variety of
music. His work has been featured on The
Source, illroots, Good Music All Day,
Pigeons and Planes and other reputable online
media outlets. Whether he is working
independently with artists or contributing
to larger productions, his vision will curate
a unique and cinematic final product.
Jackson Spilsbury also represents 1/3 of
the film production trio "Vibes N Stuff".
