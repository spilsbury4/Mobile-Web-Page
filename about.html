Jackson Spilsbury
is an independent video producer
and creative consultant who has directed
and produced visuals for many critically
acclaimed artists, stylistically catering to a wide
variety of music. His work has been featured on
Tidal, The Source, illroots, Good Music All Day,
Pigeons and Planes and other reputable online
media outlets. Whether he is working
independently with artists or contributing
to larger productions, his vision will curate
a unique and cinematic final product.
Jackson Spilsbury also represents 1/3 of
the film production trio "Vibes N Stuff".
